Dr. Dayna L. D'Acierno

Doctor of Chiropractic

Dr. D'Acierno's treatments don't include manual spinal manipulation — the "cracking" of joints traditionally associated with chiropractic care. Instead, she uses state-of-the-art instruments to provide light but optimal bone adjustments and relieve muscle tension.

Each session lasts approximately 20 minutes.

Impulse IQ®

The Smart-Device of Chiropractic

By Neuromechanical Innovations

The Impulse IQ® assists chiropractors in providing corrective yet comfortable adjustments.

Auto-Sense® Technology detects your body's response to adjustments so the IQ® knows the perfect amount of pressure to deliver.

VibraCussor®

Percussive Therapy

By IMPAC Inc.

The VibraCussor® restores muscle function by increasing circulation and decreasing muscle tightness.

Loosening tight muscles relieves joint tension and encourages proper alignment.

Manual Therapy

Deep-tissue Muscle Work

After performing the major adjustments with her tools, Dr. D'Acierno will fine-tune the treatment with her hands.

Manual treatments include trigger point therapy and myofascial release techniques to release the knots that pull on the spine.

About

Graduated from Sherman College of Chiropractic in 1988

Dr. D'Acierno is married to a retired chiropractor and has raised two children.  Her daughter graduated from Wake Forest University in 2015 and lives in Charlotte, while her son graduated from Duquesne University in 2017 and lives in the Pittsburgh area.

In her free time, Dr. D'Acierno enjoys exercise, hiking, biking, and recently even rappeling!  She maintains a healthy, organically based diet and is available for nutritional guidance and advice.
